So, what is Not in Philly all about?

Philadelphia has a litter problem. As citizens, we can make a real difference. We are a network of over 1,200 of your neighbors who have committed to cleaning their block once-a-week of litter.

Do you supply grabbers in all neighborhoods

Right now we only have funding to supply grabbers in Walnut Hill and Spruce Hill, but we are working as fast as we can to raise more. If you know of funding opportunities please pass them along, or feel free to make a donation yourself using the Donate tab.

In the meantime don't let our "grabber delay" stop you from adopting and cleaning your block, and we'll get you a grabber as soon as we can.

All I get is a grabber? Isn’t that kind of lame?

We agree. Once enough people sign up, we will be able to solicit gifts from area businesses and hold raffles to improve our rewards. Hub Bub Coffee has donated gift cards for those who post pictures of their cleaned blocks to @notinphilly and #notinphilly (if your account is public). We’ll have drawings of those who submit content while supplies last. Thank you for being a trailblazer!

What if my block is too large to cover by myself?

If you live on a large block, you can take care of just one side of the street.

Get your neighbors involved! There can be multiple participants for each block.

What if I move or am unable to complete my six month commitment?

Please keep the grabber as our gift and eliminate litter wherever you go!

How can I promote Not in Philly in my community?

You can spread the word about Not in Philly online by:

  • Liking us (and encouraging your friends and neighbors to like us) on Facebook
  • Following us on Instagram, and by tagging your own photos of Philadelphia with the #NotinPhilly hashtag
  • Following us on Twitter

You are also welcome to download and print our full page flyer or quarter-page flyer, as a way to invite neighbors to sign up!

We also need Neighborhood Champions, who can distribute grabbers within their neighborhood. If you are interested in becoming a champion, email us at notinphilly@gmail.com.
