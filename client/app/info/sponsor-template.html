We empower Philadelphians, through tech, to free their streets from litter.

Like you, we realize that Philly has a litter problem. Not In Philly provides residents with the tools needed to quickly and easily clean their block on their own schedule, asking for just a 6-month, once a week commitment to clean their neighborhood.

Not in Philly is prepared to resource any neighborhood where we ﬁnd the funding ($2,500 for a typically sized neighborhood). We are looking for Philly businesses to lend a hand in addressing this issue we all hate.

This innovative program is the ﬁrst and only map-based platform for easy street adoption. Not In Philly offers a low cost and time effective solution that easily puts to work a large number of volunteers with huge impact. Since launching in 2016, over 1000 Philadelphians from Moyamensing to Fishtown have signed up. We have press coverage from Philadelphia Magazine, The Philadelphia Inquirer and CBS-3.

What’s in it for my business?

More customers! A cleaner neighborhood is more inviting and safe. If your business is on a clean and safe street, you’re likely to attract more customers than you would otherwise.

A better bottom line! People support businesses that are truly a part of the community. Helping us clean up your neighborhood says “this is my home, too, and I care about it”

Tax deduction! All donations to Not in Philly are tax deductible.

Warm fuzzies! Doesn’t it feel good to know you’re helping to make the city better? That the neighbor- hood is a nicer place to be because of YOU? Give yourself a pat on the back, you deserve it!
